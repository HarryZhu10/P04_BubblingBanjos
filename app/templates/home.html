DESCRIPTION

App that displays a map of NYC populated with data on crime in the city over layed with additional info on the city like socioeconomic demographics. This visualization of information can help gauge the relative safety of certain areas in NYC based on various indexes.

HOW TO USE

When "Map on the top right of the screen is pressed, users will be brought to a page that displays the map. Its default location will display a part of manhattan and the surrounding area. If users wish to zoom out to get a wider view, they can scroll the mouse wheel towards themselves. If they wish to zoom in, then scroll away from themselves.

ABOUT DATA

We extracted information on the race, ethnicity, and gender demographics from the zipcodes of NYC. Then, we combined it with the frequency of motor vehicle crashes, hate crimes, shooting incidents, and arrests in those areas, which allow users to get a sense of how dangerous an area in NYC is.
